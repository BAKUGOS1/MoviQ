# MoviQ BRD — Business Requirements

## Business Objective

MoviQ should help creators and teams convert long-form video into high-quality short clips faster while keeping editorial control.

## Target Users

- Independent creators
- Agencies
- Marketing teams
- Educators
- Founders
- Media teams

## Business Requirements

- Reduce time from long-form video to short-form export.
- Improve clip selection confidence through AI explanations.
- Support repeatable team workflow through workspaces and roles.
- Provide premium editor experience that increases trust.
- Make export reliability measurable.

## KPIs

- Time to first export
- Upload completion rate
- Clip candidate acceptance rate
- Export completion rate
- Repeat project rate
- Render failure rate

## MVP Business Boundary

Focus on the core editing and export loop before monetization, marketplace or enterprise expansion.
